Enrollment Payment Agreement

Welcome to AJ Tutoring! We appreciate your choosing AJ Tutoring to prepare for the SAT/ACT/SAT-Subject examinations. We offer a short-term and intensive test preparation course that requires students to follow through on homework assignments and keep their appointments. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Your credit card will not be billed today. We bill twice a month. At the beginning of each month, your credit card will be billed for the hours completed between the 16th of the previous month and the end of the previous month. At the middle of each month, your credit card will be billed for the hours completed between the 1st of the month and the 15th of the month. We will email a paid invoice with the dates of service after each billing cycle.

If you prefer to pay by check, please make the check payable to AJ Tutoring for $1470 (10.5 hours @ $140). Pease do not send partial payment. We will send you a refund if your student does not complete 10.5 hours. If your student needs more than 10.5 hours we will mail you a second invoice at the end of the preparation.

If you have any questions about payment, please contact our bookkeeper, at (650) 331-3251 x 3, or accounting@ajtutoring.com.
